## Engagement Rules

**Within 1 Hour of Posting:**
1. Respond to ALL comments
2. Like comments from followers
3. Use thoughtful, personal responses (not generic)

**Throughout the Day:**
1. Join 5 relevant Facebook groups (art, design, travel, home decor)
2. Comment on 10+ similar artwork posts
3. Engage authentically with competitors' posts

**Weekly Reporting:**
- Track post reach, engagement rate, clicks to Etsy
- Note which post types perform best
- Adjust hashtag strategy based on performance

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't spam (1 post every 8 hours, not 3 posts every 5 minutes)
❌ Don't use generic stock images (all artwork is original)
❌ Don't make claims about artist (not you; handcrafted AI art)
❌ Don't ignore comments (engagement is CRITICAL)
❌ Don't post at random times (always 7pm for consistency)
