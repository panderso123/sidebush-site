## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary Dark:** #0a0a0a (Nearly black background)
- **Primary Gold/Accent:** #ffd700 (Bright gold highlights)
- **Secondary Gold:** #cc9900 (Darker gold for hover states)
- **Text Primary:** #ffffff (White)
- **Text Secondary:** #f2f2f2 (Off-white)
- **Text Muted:** #9a9a9a (Gray)
- **Card Background:** #141414 (Dark gray)
- **Border Color:** #2a2a2a (Subtle gray)

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** Georgia (serif)
- **Font Family Fallback:** 'Times New Roman', serif
- **Weight:** Regular (400) for body, Bold (700) for headings
- **Letter Spacing:** 1-3px for headings (uppercase), normal for body

### Visual Style
- Dark luxury aesthetic (high contrast)
- Grayscale-to-color hover transitions
- Minimalist, gallery-style layout
- High-quality imagery (300 DPI print-ready)
- Modern art gallery vibes with accessibility focus

## Product Categories

### 1. TRAVEL CITYSCAPES (16 products)
Cities rendered in surreal, dreamlike styles. Iconic landmarks with impossible lighting and color palettes.
- Examples: Florence, London, Prague, Rome, Venice, Dubai, Hong Kong, Singapore
- Formats: Print, Canvas, Apparel

### 2. SURREAL FACES (14 products)
Psychological and spiritual portrait art. Abstract faces, cosmic elements, geometric patterns.
- Examples: Meltface, Smiles & Cries, Spiral Crumbles, Monkey Forest
- Themes: Identity, emotion, spirituality, transformation
- Formats: Print, Canvas, Apparel

### 3. GARDENS & CEMETERIES (7 products)
Mystical natural spaces. Peaceful, meditative, gothic, surreal.
- Examples: Zen Garden, Cemetery Surreal, Dream Garden, Koi Garden
- Formats: Print, Canvas, Apparel

### 4. ARCHITECTURAL STUDIES (7 products)
City details and structural beauty in surreal renderings.
- Formats: Print, Canvas (framed options)

### 5. LIFESTYLE & APPAREL (6 products)
Wearable art for everyday inspiration.
- Formats: T-shirts, Hoodies, Sweatshirts, Tote Bags, All-Over Print

## Messaging Framework

### For Marketing Posts:
**Structure:** Hook + Description + CTA

**Hook Examples:**
- "This artwork will change how you see [city/concept]"
- "Imagine [city] like this..."
- "Where [concept] meets [concept]"

**CTA:** "View on Etsy" / "Shop Now" / "Save This Design"

### Product Descriptions:
- 1-2 sentence poetic description
- Color palette included
- Emotional benefit highlighted
- Use of metaphor and storytelling
